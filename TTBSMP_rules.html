Welcome to the TTBSMP rules page.

  • No Cheating/Exploiting/Hacking, if you are caught you get a 3 day ban & your inventory is wiped.

  • Combat loggging is allowed, but frowned upon.

  • Fresh spawns after day 1 get an hour of immunity from PVP unless you engage in PVP. Your immunity would no longer be valid.

  • Griefing is allowed, but you are responsible for the aftermath of the situation. (ie. The person you griefed can place a bounty on you or grief your base)

  • Only teams of up to 4 are allowed. (Alliances do not have a limit)

  • If you glitch out and you lose your items, we cannot do anything about it.

  • If anyone is live or recording, please use their nick name if they request as such.
