Full desc:

Welcome Take The Bounty SMP (TTBSMP) where the goal is to get rich off emeralds (the currency for bounties) and get power.
The server has 4 main changes compared to your average vanilla server, 1 Bounties you can place on yourself or others and tell
everyone by combining that player's head and an empty map to make a bounty poster. 2 There is one mace,
and one mace can be crafted. The first one to craft it gets it and no one else (unless they die and drop it).
Also something to keep in mind is that if a player dies and loses the mace etc: they die in the void.
The mace will then be able to get crafted again. 3 The vote concluded on a 1000,1000 world border. This may be changed in the future incase of needed room for example.
4 only a netherite pickaxe, shovel, hoe can get crafted for balancing reasons. So have fun collect those Bounties and kill one another.
